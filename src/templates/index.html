DEFINITION

$$y = (\alpha_1 {x_1}^{\rho} + \alpha_2 {x_2}^{\rho}) ^{\frac{1}{\rho}}$$
  • $\alpha_1$ and $\alpha_2$ are the shares of $x_1$ and $x_2$
  • $\rho$ is the substitution parameter
  • $\sigma = \frac{1}{1-\rho}$ is the elasticity substitution
